This post demonstrates how to track loyalty points using durable Entity Workflows in Temporal, covering accruals, tier upgrades, and redemptions for each customer. Entity Workflows provide a persistent, stateful model that survives failures without losing progress. The tutorial shows how Temporal's durable execution guarantees make loyalty systems reliable and auditable at scale.
