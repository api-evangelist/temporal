## Fetching individual pages

To fetch any page as raw Markdown, append `.md` to its URL path. For example, `https://docs.temporal.io/encyclopedia.md` returns the raw Markdown source for the Encyclopedia page.

Some pages (interactive demos, landing pages) are not available as Markdown. Requesting `.md` for those pages returns a short explanation instead.
